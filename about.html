About Me

With a strategic and results-driven approach, I have established myself as a leader in the fields of marketing and sales, demonstrating a proven track record of driving growth and enhancing market presence. My expertise spans across developing and implementing comprehensive marketing strategies, leading cross-functional teams to achieve ambitious sales targets, and leveraging data analytics to inform decision-making processes. I excel in creating and executing innovative promotional campaigns, optimising customer engagement through CRM platforms such as Zoho CRM and Salesforce, and utilising advanced digital marketing techniques to increase brand visibility and revenue.

My professional journey is characterised by significant contributions to sales efficiency, revenue growth, and customer relationship management. I have successfully navigated the complexities of business development, surpassing sales targets by developing strategic sales plans and fostering strong, lasting relationships with key stakeholders. My ability to analyse market trends and customer data has enabled me to craft targeted marketing initiatives that drive business objectives.

I am skilled in the application of a wide range of software and technologies, including advanced proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (PowerPoint, Excel, Word), as well as foundational knowledge in Python and SPSS for data analysis. These technical skills have been crucial in my ability to deliver insightful market research, effective sales presentations, and comprehensive performance reports.

Throughout my career, I have been recognized for my leadership capabilities, strategic thinking, and commitment to achieving excellence. I thrive in dynamic environments, constantly seeking innovative solutions to business challenges and fostering a culture of continuous improvement and team collaboration.

My educational background includes an MSc in International Business Management from Coventry University, United Kingdom, and an MBA in Management from the University of The Potomac, Virginia, USA. These degrees equip me with a strong foundation in business principles and a global perspective on industry challenges and opportunities.
